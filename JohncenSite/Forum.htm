Cambridge China Development Forum

Founded in 2015, the Cambridge China Development Forum (CCDF) is a student-run voluntary organization affiliated to the Chinese Students and Scholars Association in Cambridge (CSSA-CAM http://cssacam.org/about-cssa/). The CCDF’s primary mission is to promote a deeper and more comprehensive understanding of China for the public.

Events hosted by the Forum revolve around four major themes: Economics, Politics, Philosophy and Culture. The CCDF aims to organise top quality forums, conferences and talks for students, scholars and the general public in Cambridge. It also provides a platform for students, academia and professionals to discuss and exchange (share and discuss) ideas regarding China’s development.

Powered by Cambridge China Development Forum, University of Cambridge, England.
